# Privacy Policy for PatientLog

**Last Updated:** December 24, 2025

## Introduction

PatientLog ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Email address, name, and credentials for authentication
- **Professional Information**: Doctor specialization, clinic details
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers

### Patient Data (Created by You)
- **Patient Records**: Names, contact information, medical notes, visit history
- **Clinical Notes**: Diagnosis, treatment plans, prescriptions (stored locally and synced)
- **Images**: Profile photos or clinical images uploaded by you

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: App features used, session duration, crash reports
- **Technical Data**: IP address, browser type, device information

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the app functionality
- Authenticate your account and ensure security
- Sync your data across devices
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Send important updates about the service
- Respond to your support requests

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Patient data is stored **locally** on your device using encrypted WatermelonDB
- You control when data is synced to our servers

### Cloud Storage
- Data is synced to secure MongoDB servers with encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Data at rest is encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption
- Backups are performed regularly with encrypted storage

### Security Measures
- JWT-based authentication with secure token management
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and user authentication
- Encrypted data transmission

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **DO NOT**:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share patient data with advertisers
- Use patient data for marketing purposes

We **MAY** share information:
- With cloud service providers (AWS, MongoDB Atlas) for hosting and backup
- When required by law or valid legal process
- To protect rights, property, or safety
- With your explicit consent

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Export**: Download your patient data in portable format
- **Withdrawal**: Revoke consent for data processing

## Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data retained as long as account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
- Backups: Retained for 90 days for disaster recovery

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Expo**: App development and deployment platform
- **MongoDB Atlas**: Database hosting
- **Sentry**: Error tracking and monitoring

Each service has its own privacy policy governing data use.

## Children's Privacy

PatientLog is not intended for use by anyone under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.
